Dress Code

We are at the ballpark so it's not a formal affair. Here is some guidance:

Women:

Casual summer dresses / skirts and comfy sandals

Men:

Chinos or khakis & golf shirts or buttondowns

No Blazer. No ties. No jeans. No athletic clothes.

HOWEVER—Favorite sports team's caps are encouraged! ⚾

Transportation & Timing

It's a big ballpark, leave yourself a little extra time. The ceremony starts at 4:00 pm.

Drop-off:

  • Gate C at 21st and Blake
  • Uber or Lyft can drop you off right there
  • Kids can be dropped off right outside Gate C also
  • Coors Field Staff will be there to ensure all guests can find the ceremony

Pick-up:

  • Also at Gate C - 21st and Blake
  • Plan for 9:30-10:00

Driving/Parking

If you are driving, you will have free parking in Lot A

GPS Address: 1601 Park Ave West, Denver, CO 80205

Enter the Coors Field Parking Lot A from Park Ave and Wazee

Directions from I-25:

  1. Take the Park Ave exit and head east on Park Ave.
  2. Stay in the far-left lane on Park Avenue and turn left at Wazee Street. You will see the stadium on your right side.
  3. Proceed through the next stoplight into the Lot A parking area.
  4. At the base of the ramp, make a left turn and drive toward Coors Field.
  5. On non-game days, handicap parking is not enforced ~ please park as close to the stadium as possible including in handicap spots.
  6. After parking, walk towards the ballpark and go up the stairs to the left of Gate A. Proceed around the outside of the ballpark to Gate C.
