About the Venue

Eli's Be Mitzvah celebration will take place at the iconic Coors Field, home of the Colorado Rockies. This beautiful ballpark in downtown Denver offers stunning views of the Rocky Mountains and the city skyline, creating the perfect backdrop for this special occasion.

Game Day Uniform: Dressy Casual

  • No ties
  • No tanks
  • No tracksuits
  • Favorite sports team's caps encouraged

Parking & Transportation

Coors Field offers several parking options in the surrounding area. We recommend the following:

  • Coors Field Parking Lots: Several lots are available directly adjacent to the ballpark.
  • Downtown Parking: Numerous public parking garages are within walking distance.
  • Ride-sharing: Uber and Lyft are convenient options with designated drop-off areas.
  • Public Transportation: The RTD Light Rail has a stop at Union Station, a short walk from Coors Field.

For guests staying at nearby hotels, many are within walking distance or offer shuttle services to downtown attractions.
